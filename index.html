MENTORED

PROJECT

A cooperative project among

Federal University of Minas Gerais,

University of São Paulo, Federal University of Pernambuco,

Federal Institute of Santa Catarina, University of Itajaí Valley and

Rede Nacional de Pesquisa

A research project selected in the MCTIC/CGI/FAPESP 2018 public call on

Strategic Research for the Internet organized by

São Paulo State Science Foundation (FAPESP)

About

The project has the following main goals:

  1. Identify, model, and evaluate malicious behavior on the Internet of Things (IoT);
  2. Help in the construction of advanced and coordinated solutions to enable: prevention, prediction, detection, and mitigation of DDoS attacks;
  3. Provide to the scientific community in Cybersecurity a testbed to allow researchers to experiment their solutions regarding to DDoS attacks.
